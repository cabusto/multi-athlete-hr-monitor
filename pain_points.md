Problem: Coaches lack real-time visibility into internal load across a group
What it is: A visibility tool for group coaching decisions
Pitch: “It gives coaches a live view of how the whole group is actually handling the workout — not just how it looks.”

The 3 strongest pain points:
1. Can’t see everyone at once
2. Recovery is invisible
3. Asking athletes is slow + unreliable


Quotes:
“I can’t track everyone at once”
Impact: Coaches go from selective attention → group awareness

“I don’t actually know who’s overcooking it”
Coaches can catch overreaching early, not after the session

“Recovery is a black box”
Recovery becomes visible and comparable

“Asking everyone for HR is slow and unreliable”
Removes friction + inconsistency + delay

“I lose context across the group”
Enables true comparison, not memory-based comparison

“I don’t know who needs adjustment right now”
Enables targeted coaching in real time

“Group sessions don’t scale well”
Coaches can maintain quality with larger groups

“I miss subtle fatigue patterns”
Coaches catch fatigue before breakdown

“I can’t validate if the session is hitting the intended effort”
Coaches can confirm: “This is actually a threshold session”

“I spend too much mental energy tracking details”
Frees cognitive load for actual coaching
